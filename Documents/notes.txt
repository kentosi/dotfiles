Fonts: fc-list

File manager: ranger

Hibernate: pm-suspend

Screen tearing: added here:
/usr/share/X11/xorg.conf.d/20-intel.conf:
---
Section "Device"
  Identifier  "Intel Graphics"
  Driver      "intel"
  Option      "TearFree" "true"
EndSection

screen stuff: xrandr and arandr 
switching screens:
xrandr --output HDMI1 --auto --output eDP1 --off
^ set to --auto or --off
